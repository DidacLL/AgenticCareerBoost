Entries

I write when something in the work keeps annoying me after the terminal is closed. Sometimes it is a tool. Sometimes it is a pattern everyone repeats with a straight face. Sometimes it is my own repository getting messy enough to deserve a cleanup note.

The entries are small because the site is small. They are not summaries of a brand strategy. They are the place where I can keep a thought with the project that produced it.
