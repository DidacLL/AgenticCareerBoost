# S-001.5 UOC Fair Target Shortlist

- **Sprint**: S-001.5
- **Date**: 2026-04-27
- **Event**: VI Fira Virtual d'Ocupacio de la UOC / VI Feria Virtual de Empleo de la UOC
- **Sources**: UOC fair page, JobTeaser event page, S-001 Barcelona market research, S-001 positioning synthesis

## Event Facts

The fair runs **28, 29 and 30 April 2026**. It is online and inside the UOC Career Center / JobTeaser environment. The event page lists **123 companies present**, job and internship offers, company profiles, virtual interview requests, talks, orientation sessions, and networking spaces.

The fair page explicitly says participants should update their Career Center profile to show their potential before the event. That makes public profile cleanup a direct event-readiness task, not cosmetic work.

## Ranking Rules

Priority scoring uses the S-001 positioning decision:

1. **Primary**: ML/Data Engineering with domain depth.
2. **Secondary**: Agentic systems, tooling, documentation, platform thinking.
3. **Tertiary**: research engineering / applied AI.
4. **Deprioritize**: body-shop consulting, generic CRUD, weak learning slope.

## Interview Questions To Use

- What junior or student-accessible engineering roles are open during or after the fair?
- Is the role product/team-based or client-assignment based?
- What would I build or maintain in the first three months?
- Is there a data, ML, platform, tooling, or automation path for this role?
- How do you evaluate GitHub or public project evidence in early-career candidates?
- Can I send a short project page showing AgenticCareerBoost and P3CTeX as evidence?

## Acceptance Notes

- Do not apply to every company. Prioritize 8-12 targeted contacts.
- For consulting firms, ask screening questions before spending application effort.
- Do not lead with "career changer." Lead with UOC ML/AI, inspectable systems, and regulated-domain discipline.
