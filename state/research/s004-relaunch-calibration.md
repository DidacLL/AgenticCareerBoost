## Correction applied

The rejected drift was a generic market-driven profile: backend, data quality,
BI/reporting, and boring data operations. That direction conflicts with the
project identity and with the user correction.

The preserved direction is:

- agentic systems as differentiator;
- research/applied AI as high-fit lane;
- ML/data systems only when engineering-heavy and creative;
- backend/platform only as product-company fallback;
- public proof through repository architecture, formal documentation, reports,
  reviews, and campaign artifacts.

## Role filter

High fit:

- AI workflow engineer;
- applied AI / research engineer;
- junior AI software research engineer;
- ML systems engineer;
- developer tooling engineer;
- platform engineer in a product or R&D team;
- technical documentation / engineering systems hybrid roles.

Conditional fit:

- data engineer, only when it includes automation, pipelines, APIs, modeling,
  platform ownership, or research proximity;
- backend engineer, only in product companies and only when it supports systems,
  platform, infrastructure, AI, fintech, or regulated-domain complexity.

Low fit / reject:

- data quality analyst;
- BI reporting maintainer;
- dashboard-heavy analyst;
- CRUD-only backend junior;
- outsourcing/staff augmentation;
- unpaid internship;
- equity-only startup;
- AI prompt-content role without engineering ownership.

## Company direction

Priority targets from the documented strategy remain valid as categories:

- research and R&D: BSC-CNS, i2CAT, CVC, UOC IN3, Eurecat, UPC-linked teams;
- AI/R&D product: HP Sant Cugat, Red Points, Dynatrace, Worldsensing;
- product companies with systems depth: Typeform, Wallapop, Factorial, TravelPerk;
- regulated-domain product/tech: CaixaBank Tech, N26, Qonto, seQura.

Companies are not accepted just because they have public junior roles. They must
pass the role filter and avoid the CRUD/data-quality trap.

## Avoidance rules preserved

Do not apply first-pass to:

- Capgemini, Accenture, NTT Data, Indra, Sopra Steria in staff-augmentation mode;
- Inetum, Aubay, Altran, NEORIS junior CRUD tiers;
- unpaid internships;
- relocation-required roles outside Barcelona;
- offers below the documented exploitation threshold;
- pre-seed equity-only startups.

A named company can still be considered if a specific role clearly breaks the
bad pattern. The role description must prove product ownership, R&D depth, or
engineering autonomy.
